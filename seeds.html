SUNFLOWER SEEDS

Sunflower seeds are a powerhouse of nutrition, packed with vitamin E, magnesium, and healthy fats. These small yet mighty seeds support heart health, improve skin glow, and boost your immune system naturally.
Crunchy and flavorful, sunflower seeds are rich in antioxidants, fiber, and plant-based protein. Enjoy them roasted or raw as a wholesome snack or add them to salads and trail mixes for a nutritious twist.
